# 📊 Retail Sales Performance & Executive Dashboard (Excel)

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Excel dashboard designed to analyze retail sales performance, profitability, customer trends, and regional performance. The dashboard enables business stakeholders to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs) and make data-driven decisions.

---

## Business Problem

Organizations often struggle to track sales performance across products, regions, and customer segments. Manual reporting is time-consuming and makes it difficult to identify growth opportunities.

The objective of this project was to build a centralized Excel dashboard that provides quick access to important business metrics and insights.

* Monthly Sales Trend

### Profit Analysis

* Profit by Category
* Profit by Region
* Top Performing Products

### Customer Analysis

* Customer Segments
* Revenue Contribution
* Purchase Trends

---

## Key Business Insights

* A small number of products contribute the majority of revenue.
* Technology products generate strong profitability.
* Regional performance varies significantly.
* Seasonal trends impact sales growth.
* Customer purchasing behavior differs across segments.
